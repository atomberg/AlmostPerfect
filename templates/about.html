Hi, I'm Alex Tomberg. This app was developed in four weeks while I was an Insight Data Science Fellow in NYC. You can see my presentation about this app or check out its source code at GitHub.

If you want to get in touch, here is my email and my LinkedIn profile.

Algorithm core:
Reviews are retrieved from the database and tokenized into unigrams and bigrams. The tokens are then scored using mutual information metric to detect those that are most associated with negative reviews. The rest is bells and whistles.
Data:
513k Amazon laptop and camera reviews from a collection of raw JSON files, stored in a MySQL database.
Backend:
Python, pandas, numpy, scikit-learn and NLTK to do the heavy lifting; SQLalchemy for interfacing with MySQL.
Frontend:
Flask, Bootstrap, D3 & JavaScript, all deployed on AWS.
